About the Practitioner

Hello, I'm Glad You're Here

I am a trained Practical Homeopath (PHom), having completed professional education through the Academy of Practical Homeopathy.

My passion is supporting mothers and families as they learn how to confidently use practical homeopathy in their homes.

My approach is gentle, respectful, and rooted in education. Sessions focus on understanding, learning, and empowerment — never diagnosis or medical treatment.

Ambassador for Practical Homeopathy

I am also an Ambassador for Discover Practical Homeopathy, helping to share educational awareness of practical homeopathy principles.

My goal is to walk alongside families as they build confidence and clarity, one small step at a time.
