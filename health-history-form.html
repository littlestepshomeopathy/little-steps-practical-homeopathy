Health History Questionnaire

Chief Complaints and Health History Questionnaire

Little Steps to Healing Homeopathy

Please complete this questionnaire before your session. This information helps guide our educational conversation. It is not a medical evaluation.

1. Chief Complaints

What is your chief complaint? Please briefly list the ailment(s).

2. Onset

When did you first have this/these complaint(s), even in a very mild form?

3. Diagnosis

What, if any, diagnosis was made? What diagnostic tests were performed?

4. Health Professionals Consulted

Did you consult a health professional? If so, what is their specialty?

5. Family Health History

Please list any known history of disease in your family such as cancer, heart disease, diabetes, alcoholism, etc. What is your relationship? If deceased, what was your parents', grandparents', siblings' cause of death?

6. Treatments & Medical Events Timeline

Please list any treatments and/or noteworthy medical events. Specific dates are not important; approximate as best you can. List from present to past in chronological order.

*Please include dental history such as any extractions, root canals, or wisdom teeth removal.
